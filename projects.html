Projects

Proposal

This proposal was written for an English Composition 2 class from Arapahoe Community College. We were tasked to write a proposal to someone to pose and offer as a solution to an existing problem. I chose to try and find a solution based on the actions implemented by others. I narrowed down on the diminishing fertility rates of the world specificaly the United States. My proposal was for the state of New Mexico but was not required to be submitted by the end of class. This paper was worked over the entirety of the semester and submitted as the final paper.


Ethical Anaylsis

This project was a analytical anaylsis of the ethical theories that Michael Tooley presented in his paper "In defense of abortion and infanticide." We analyzed his paper based on the ethical theories we learned from the class. This paper took place over the course of the semester. This paper tested our analytical skills to see if we could apply theories that Tooley used in his work.
