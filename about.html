More About Me

I am a Colorado native and am pretty big on getting out to the mountains and surrounding areas. I love to fly fish the Platte or the Colorado River and anything else local. I also enjoy hiking and walking around in nature to take in the silence and beauty of Gods creation. I like getting outside and playing volleyball with friends and family. I also love volunteering with Military Living History groups, Tenth Mountain Division Living History Display Group, Inc. and American Military Living history Association We have done displays at the anual 40s Ball in Boulder, Broomfield Veterans Museum , and Vetrans and Memorial Day parades and services across Colorado.
